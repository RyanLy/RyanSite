Sudoku Voices

An Android application coded in Eclipse Android Studio. Sudoku Voices is not your ordinary Sudoku application. It's Sudoku that can speak and understand you! Come and give it a try on your Android Device. For best results for the speech features, I would recommend Android Jelly Bean(4.1) and up. Sudoku Voices is currently on the Google Play Store. Feel free to try it out. Please email me any suggestions that you may have!


WardTracker

A web application coded in a MEAN JavaScript stack. This application is designed for the world's most popular video game, League of Legends. This application provides a simple interface to compare the number of wards placed in their last 10 game sessions. This makes it easy to compare how often a player wards relative to other players.
