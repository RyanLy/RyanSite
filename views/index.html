I'm Ryan and I'm a 2nd year student studying Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I'm currently a developer at a startup called Wriber located in Kitchener, Ontario for my co-op term. We hope to help writers everywhere overcome their Writers' Block by providing them with suggestions on what to write about. We try to solve problems in natural language processing.

I'm also working on an Sudoku application on Android. I'm currently experimenting with Google's voice input and recognition APIs, so feel free to check it out.
